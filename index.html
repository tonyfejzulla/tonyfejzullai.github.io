What daytime is about:

We are working on creating a multiplatform software that can give real-time feedback of what is going on on every task, you as a team manager, have issued to your team.

All started with the concept of grouping and automating into one single system, all those things we do in order to take charge, develop, accomplish and report our work tasks.

It can be a project we are working on, a customer support job, an on site appointment, or just a simple scheduled task.

Everything is going to be transparent to everyone including the empolyer, the emplyees and even the customer.

Never get lost anymore over hundreds of calls and emails, with our system, everything you need to boost your business productivity is going to be just one click away.
